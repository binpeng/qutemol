How to cite QuteMol:

If you use images made with QuteMol in a scientific paper or book:

cite the scientific paper that presented QuteMol:

Marco Tarini, Paolo Cignoni, Claudio Montani:
Ambient Occlusion and Edge Cueing for Enhancing Real Time Molecular Visualization
IEEE Transactions on Visualization and Computer Graphics Volume 12 , Issue 5 , Pages 1237-1244 , 2006 , ISSN:1077-2626

Bibtex entry (for LaTeX users):

@article{1187858,
  author = {Marco Tarini and Paolo Cignoni and Claudio Montani},
  title = {Ambient Occlusion and Edge Cueing for Enhancing Real Time Molecular Visualization},
  journal = {IEEE Transactions on Visualization and Computer Graphics},
  volume = {12},
  number = {5},
  year = {2006},
  issn = {1077-2626},
  pages = {1237--1244},
  doi = {http://dx.doi.org/10.1109/TVCG.2006.115},
  publisher = {IEEE Educational Activities Department},
  address = {Piscataway, NJ, USA},
}

if you want, you can report the official QuteMOl website too (see below).

If you use images made with QuteMol in a webpage:

be sure to acknowledge that fact, and also to put a link to the official QuteMol website: http://qutemol.sourceforge.net
